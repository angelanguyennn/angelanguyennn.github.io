Paragraph

21-year-old producer and visual artist TEMPOREX (real name Joseph Flores) is bringing the wacky CGI visuals of bowling back into vogue in his latest album, aptly titled Bowling. Boasting over 70 million streams on Spotify alone, TEMPOREX hones in on nostalgic sounds inspired by Prince, Jai Paul and 80s synthesizers to craft unique, dreamy synth-pop tracks.
