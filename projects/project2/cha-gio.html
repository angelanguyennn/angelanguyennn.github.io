Chả Giò

Ingredients

  • 1 package Menlo brand egg roll wrappers
  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 1 medium jícama (about 1/2 cup)
  • 2/3 cup onion chopped
  • 1/2 cup bean thread noodle cut into 1″-1.5″ threads
  • 1/8 cup wood ear mushrooms chopped
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp sugar
    • WRAPPING SEALER:
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tbsp flour

Instructional Video

About

Chả giò, also known as fried spring roll, is a popular dish in Vietnamese cuisine and usually served as an appetizer in Europe and North America, where there are large Vietnamese diaspora. It is ground meat, usually pork, wrapped in rice paper and deep-fried.
